### What is Healthcare Translator?
Healthcare Translator is a real-time translation app that helps patients and healthcare providers communicate across language barriers. It converts speech to text, translates it, and can speak the translation out loud.

### Selecting Languages

**For Healthcare Providers:**
1. **FROM dropdown**: Select the patient's language
2. **TO dropdown**: Select your language (English, typically)
3. Click the **â‡„ button** between dropdowns to swap languages quickly

**Example Setup:**
- Patient speaks Spanish â†’ FROM: Spanish
- Provider speaks English â†’ TO: English

### Starting a Conversation

1. **Click "Start Listening" button** (blue microphone button)
   - The button turns red and shows "Stop Listening"
   - A status message appears: "Listening... Speak now"

2. **Speak clearly into your microphone**
   - Speak at normal pace, not too fast or slow
   - Pause briefly between sentences
   - Gray text = app is still processing (interim)
   - Black text = final transcription confirmed

3. **Watch the translation appear**
   - Original speech appears in LEFT panel
   - Translation appears in RIGHT panel (within 1-2 seconds)
   - Translation updates automatically as you speak

### Playing Audio Translation

1. **Click the "Speak" button** (green button with speaker icon)
   - Located in the top-right of the translation panel
   - Audio plays automatically
   - Button shows "Playing..." during playback

2. **Listen to the translation**
   - Natural-sounding voice
   - Slightly slower pace for clarity
   - Wait for audio to finish before speaking again

### Clearing the Screen

- **Click "Clear" button** to remove all text and start fresh
- Useful when starting a new topic or patient consultation

### Stopping Recording

- **Click "Stop Listening"** (red button) to stop speech recognition
- Microphone stops listening immediately
- You can start again by clicking "Start Listening"

## ğŸ’¡ Best Practices

### For Healthcare Providers

**Before the Appointment:**
- Test microphone and speakers
- Verify languages are correctly selected
- Close unnecessary browser tabs for better performance

**During Consultation:**
- Speak one person at a time
- Pause after each question or statement
- Wait for translation to complete before continuing
- Use "Speak" button to play translation for patient

- Click "Clear" to remove sensitive information
- Close browser tab when done

### For Patients

**Speaking Tips:**
- Speak clearly and at normal pace
- Face the microphone (usually built into computer/tablet)
- Minimize background noise
- Wait for your turn to speak

**Understanding Translation:**
- Listen carefully to audio playback
- Ask provider to repeat if unclear
- Use simple, direct language for best results

## âš ï¸ Troubleshooting

### Microphone Not Working
**Problem**: App says "Microphone access denied"
**Solution**:
1. Check browser permissions (usually top-left of address bar)
2. Click lock icon â†’ Site settings â†’ Microphone â†’ Allow
3. Refresh page

### No Translation Appearing
**Problem**: Original text shows but no translation
**Solution**:
1. Wait 1-2 seconds (translation has slight delay)
2. Check status bar for error messages
3. Verify internet connection
4. Ensure correct languages are selected

### Audio Not Playing
**Problem**: "Speak" button clicked but no sound
**Solution**:
1. Check device volume
2. Verify speakers/headphones connected
3. Try again - first playback may take longer
4. Check browser audio permissions

### Poor Transcription Accuracy
**Problem**: Words are incorrect or missing
**Solution**:
1. Speak more slowly and clearly
2. Reduce background noise
3. Move closer to microphone
4. Speak one sentence at a time
5. Use Chrome or Edge browser

### Language Not Detected
**Problem**: Wrong language detected
**Solution**:
1. Manually select correct language in dropdown
2. Speak more clearly
3. Use common phrases first
